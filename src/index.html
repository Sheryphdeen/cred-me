 We help grow small and medium scale enterprise

Our Goal is to Help Business Grow.

Lifting traders and business owners out of poverty through sustaining and scaling their business is our Major Priortiy.

Cred ME raises fund from other international NGOs, the Ministry of Trade and Commerce with other dedicated philantropists.
The Loans range from as low as $20 to $500 depending on a lot of factors; size of business, vulnerability and need, each sucessful loan repayment qualifies the business to acquire a higher loan.
Cred ME was established 6 years ago, which we have impacted directly 718 business with over $500,000 and more to the process of scaling business.
